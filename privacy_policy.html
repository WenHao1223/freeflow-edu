Privacy Policy for FreeFlow Edu

At FreeFlow Edu, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.   1. Information Collection:

  2. Use of Information:

  3. Information Sharing:

  4. Security Measures:

    • We prioritize the security of your personal information and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
    • Data transmission is secured through encryption methods to ensure confidentiality.

  5. Cookies and Tracking:

  6. Access and Control:

    • Users have the right to access, modify, or delete their personal information upon request.
    • Special measures are taken to accommodate the specific needs of individuals with disabilities to ensure their access and control over their information.

  8. Compliance:

    • We comply with relevant data protection laws and regulations in the jurisdictions where we operate.
