Data Deletion Policy for FreeFlow Edu:

At FreeFlow Edu, we are committed to protecting your privacy and providing control over your personal information. This Data Deletion Policy outlines the procedures and guidelines for the deletion of user data on our platform.

  1. User Data Removal Request:

    • Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data stored on FreeFlow Edu. Such requests can be made by contacting our support team at [wenhao1223@student.usm.com].

  2. Verification of Identity:

    • To ensure the security of user data, we may require verification of the identity of the individual making the deletion request.

  3. Data Categories Subject to Deletion:

    • Upon receiving a deletion request, we will proceed to delete personal information, including but not limited to user account details, contact information, and any content or contributions provided by the user.

  4. Deletion Process:

    • FreeFlow Edu will take all reasonable steps to promptly delete the requested data. However, certain information may be retained for legal or administrative purposes, as permitted by law.

  5. Data Retention Period:

    • Some data may be retained for a specific period in compliance with legal, accounting, or regulatory requirements even after the user data deletion request.

  6. Deletion Confirmation:

    • Upon completion of the deletion process, a confirmation will be sent to the user's provided contact information to ensure that the requested data has been erased.

  7. Exceptions to Deletion:

    • Certain information might not be deleted if retention is necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our Terms and Conditions.

  8. Deletion of Third-party Information:

    • FreeFlow Edu will not be responsible for the deletion of information shared by users with third parties or individuals outside of our platform.

  9. Requests from Special Needies:

    • Special accommodations and assistance will be provided to individuals with special needs, refugees, and those with disabilities who require support in the data deletion process.

  10. Updates to the Deletion Policy:

  • FreeFlow Edu reserves the right to update this Data Deletion Policy. Any changes will be communicated through the platform or via user contact information.

At FreeFlow Edu, we respect your right to manage your personal data. If you have any questions or concerns regarding the deletion of your data, please contact us at [wenhao1223@student.usm.com].
